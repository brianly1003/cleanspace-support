## Description of Service

CleanSpace is a mobile application designed to help users:
- Analyze device storage usage
- Find and remove duplicate photos using machine learning
- Manage screenshots and large media files
- Securely store private photos with encryption
- Find and merge duplicate contacts
- Convert Live Photos to still images
- Compress videos to save space

All processing occurs entirely on your device. Your personal content (photos, videos, contacts, files) never leaves your device.
